# Audience: Dreamforce Staffing Optimization

## Primary Audience

### Summer Hash - New Staffing Lead

**Role:** Dreamforce 2026 Staffing Lead  
**Background:** New to this role, first time managing Dreamforce staffing  
**Needs:**
- Clear understanding of what the job entails
- Visibility into current state at all times
- Tools that reduce manual work
- Confidence that she won't miss critical tasks
- Quick answers to "what should I do next?"

**Pain Points:**
- Overwhelmed by complexity of the role
- Worried about making mistakes
- Limited time (has other responsibilities)
- Doesn't have prior year's context

**Success Looks Like:**
- She feels confident managing the process
- She spends her time on high-value decisions, not manual data entry
- She can answer questions quickly (current state, problems, timeline)
- She knows when things are going wrong early enough to fix them

**Communication Preferences:**
- Clear, concise documentation (not walls of text)
- Visual dashboards over reports
- Action-oriented (what to do) over academic (why things work)
- Just-in-time information (what she needs now, not everything at once)

## Communication Strategy by Audience

### For Summer
- **Format:** Clear documentation, visual dashboards, step-by-step guides
- **Tone:** Supportive and confidence-building
- **Frequency:** Regular check-ins, just-in-time info
- **Channel:** Direct (Slack DMs, docs shared directly)

### For Domain Experts (Dustin, James, Ria, Marika's team)
- **Format:** Specific questions, structured interviews
- **Tone:** Respectful of their expertise and time
- **Frequency:** As-needed, batched to respect their time
- **Channel:** Scheduled calls/meetings, async documentation

### For Stakeholders (Alyssa, Strategic Events)
- **Format:** Executive summaries, key decisions
- **Tone:** Professional, outcomes-focused
- **Frequency:** Major milestones only
- **Channel:** Email, brief meetings

### For Staff (Attendees)
- **Format:** Simple instructions, FAQs, self-service tools
- **Tone:** Friendly and clear
- **Frequency:** When they need to take action
- **Channel:** Email, Slack announcements, self-service portal

## Accessibility Needs

- **Time-constrained:** Everyone has limited time, respect it
- **New to role:** Summer needs extra context and support
- **Different expertise levels:** Technical details for some, high-level for others
- **Mobile access:** Summer may need to check things during event on phone
- **Async-friendly:** Not everyone available for real-time collaboration
